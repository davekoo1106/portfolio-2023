Project Overview

Rive is a performance and wellness company which focuses primarily on providing premium natural supplements pertaining to protein poweders, pre workout and essential vitamins.

Introduction

The goal of this project was to create a simple yet pleasing design for a supplement company using Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Dimension to showcase realistic packaging for the brand.

Rive's Beginning

Logo Process

I started the logo process by coming up with a few rough ideas as to what I wanted my logo to look like. I knew from the start that I wanted to incorporate 'Rive' within my logo.

From there, I came up with ways to enhance the project without overdoing it. I experimented with ways to incorporate shapes within the logo. I concluded that alternating designs for the "V" gave optimum results

Therefore, the images below demonstrate my process of experimenting with various shapes, sizes, and colour schemes in designing my logo. Expanding on color choice I decided that I wanted to use blue as it evokes senses of trustworthiness and security as I want customers to have a sense of legitmacy and proficiency

Comparing to many main stream companies that often disclose certain information on their products, the highlight of RIVE pertains to its full transparencyof their ingredients and sources

Labels

After logo design, I created two different versions of labels that may be applied to the packaging of the product:

Version 1

The first version includes important information such as the nutritional values, the flavour, the branding and logo as well as the portion servings and size

Version 2

The second version was essentially a revision from the first version

Main differences within this version specifically addresses alternations towards the logo, explanding color schemes for the packaging and enhancements on graphics

Changes to the logo were made to improve overall display and originality of the product. With creative innovation, the logo will project towards a design that customers will remember better
