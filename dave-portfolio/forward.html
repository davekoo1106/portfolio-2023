Project Overview

Forward is a mentorship program that was awarded first-place at BCITs Hack the Break 2023.

Introduction

Problem Statement

How can individuals facing job insecurity proactively develop their skills and knowledge to increase their employability and resilience in the labor market?

Solution

Inspired by our own struggles with our job search journey. We know how valuable and beneficial someone's guidance and encouragement can be for someone just starting out in the tech industry.

Forward provides a platform that empowers individuals to achieve their full potential. Connect to local mentors to help guide their personal and professional journeys.

Challenges

The biggest challenge that we ran into was figuring out how we can create the product forward in the given time frame. In the beginning, it was overwheleming with the amount of tasks that needed to be done, however, we came together as a team to break down the most important tasks and evenutally we were able to complete the project
